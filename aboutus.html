A Little About Us

Gracie and Alyssa are both former teachers. They met while teaching together in Guatemala City. After finishing their contracts abroad, both Gracie and Alyssa experienced interesting culture shocks of coming back to the United States to teach. Gracie left the classroom first and found a job in Edtech. Alyssa was inspired by Gracie's story and reached out to her to ask questions about how the heck she did it! After a few conversations together, the realization that so many more teachers are wondering the same thing hit hard. They decided to do Monthly calls to keep each other motivated to reach their own goals. Alyssa was in a program learning web development at that time and decided to build a website for bringing former and aspiring-former teachers together in a mentorship-style program so that they could start connecting like we had been able to.

Our Mission

We believe that teachers are some of the hardest working people out there. We also know how taxing the work is and how toxic the school environment can be. We want to be a support systems for teachers who are ready to move on. But the process of leaving a teaching career is intimidating, confusing, and overwhelming. We aim to make this process less daunting and to provide you with a community and support system of fellow teachers who want to leave, along with former teachers who wish to aid in that process.
